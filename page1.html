Facts and Figures about World Hunger.

Facts and Figures

821 MILLION

The number of people worldwide classified as malnourished in 2017.




22%

The global percentage of children under 5 who are malnourished.




66%

The percentage of global malnourishment that comes from these two regions: Sub-Saharan Africa (237 Million) Southern Asia (277 Million).




40 MILLION

The number of children under 5 who are overweight.

45%

The percentage of deaths of children under 5 each year due to poor nutrition.




66%

The percentage of extremely poor employed workers who are agricultural workers.




149 MILLION

The amount of children under 5 who are stunted.




49 MILLION

The amount of children under 5 who are affected by food waste.
